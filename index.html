Research

Job market paper

Borders Within: Citizenship and the Paradox of Belonging for Turkish-Origin Youth

Job Market Paper · Draft available upon request

This paper studies the effects of Germany’s 2000 Nationality Act on second-generation Turkish-origin youth. Using a difference-in-differences design around birthright citizenship eligibility, I examine whether access to citizenship fosters belonging and educational integration, or whether it can also sharpen perceptions of exclusion in selective institutional environments.
